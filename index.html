How to connect Gemini
1. Get a free API key from Google AI Studio.
2. Paste it in the field above and click Save.
3. The status dot in the top bar turns green when a key is present.
4. All API calls go directly from your browser to generativelanguage.googleapis.com.
5. Chronis never proxies or logs your key or your data.
